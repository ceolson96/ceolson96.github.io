Outfit Website (Asp.net MVC)

Project information

This project was developed for my outfit (group within the bigger organization Corps of Cadets) at Texas A&M. It was used to keep track of if a cadet would be present not at a given event that the Corps of Cadets had. We decided to make this site so that we had the ability to access and submit the passes online without having to physically give paper passes or take the accountablility of all cadets in the outfit on paper. This project was to keep our outfit more organized and efficient with our activities.
